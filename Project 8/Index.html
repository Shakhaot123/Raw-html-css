How We Can Help

Carefully Planned Events

From your initial contact, we are here with you every step of the way in helping to plan your event. We'll work together on an internal CAD diagram of your marquee, finalising a layout that works for everyone so you can plan your seating arrangements and visualise the space. At a time convenient with you, we'll come out for a site visit to meet you in person, view the area you plan to have the marquee and discuss any queries face to face.

When you book with us your event is fully covered with our total support promise. This means we have spare equipment, furniture, crew and vehicles ready whenever you need them. Come rain or shine we’re totally committed to ensure your event is the best it can be
